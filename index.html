Travelmagic

Support

Travelmagic is the living daily itinerary for solo travelers. Tell it when you land, and it builds your day — then re-adjusts around delays and the gaps in between.

Contact

Questions, bugs, or ideas? Email your-support-email@example.com. We read every message.

Is it free?

Yes — Travelmagic is completely free right now.

Is my data private?

Your trips, notes, and photos are stored on your device. Only your trip details (city, dates, times) are sent to an AI service to generate your itinerary. See our Privacy Policy for the full picture.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all of its data from your device.

Which languages are supported?

Korean and English — switch anytime from the top-right toggle inside the app.

무료인가요?

제 데이터는 안전한가요?

여행·메모·사진은 기기에 저장됩니다. 일정 생성 시에는 여행 정보(도시·날짜·시각)만 AI 서비스로 전송됩니다. 자세한 내용은 개인정보처리방침을 참고하세요.

데이터를 어떻게 지우나요?

앱을 삭제하면 기기에서 모든 데이터가 함께 삭제됩니다.

어떤 언어를 지원하나요?

한국어와 영어 — 앱 안 우상단 토글에서 언제든 전환할 수 있어요.
